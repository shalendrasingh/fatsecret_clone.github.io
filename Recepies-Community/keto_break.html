Keto Bread

by member: queenisreeni

Keto bread with almond flour.

Ingredients

6 medium egg(whole)
60 g butter
3 tsps baking powder(low sodium)
1 tsps cream of tartar
1 dash sea salt
168 g almond flour

Directions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.

  2. Whip egg whites with the cream of tartar.

  3. Flod in 1/3 of the egg and butter with dry ingredients.

  4. Flol in the rest of the egg whites.

  5. Bake in 30 min.
